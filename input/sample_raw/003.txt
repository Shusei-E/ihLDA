Sport climbing gym offers discounted rates for teenage program
A popular sport climbing gym is offering discounted rates for its new teenage program. The gym, located in the downtown area, hopes to attract more teenagers to the sport and promote a healthy lifestyle. The program offers a range of activities, including bouldering, lead climbing, and top-rope climbing, and is open to all skill levels. The gym's management hopes that the discounted rates will encourage more teenagers to join the program and develop their climbing skills.
