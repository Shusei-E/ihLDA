Sport climbing gym partners with local schools for teenage program
A sport climbing gym has partnered with several local schools to promote its teenage program. The program, which was launched earlier this year, is designed to help teenagers develop their climbing skills and promote a healthy lifestyle. The gym's management hopes that by partnering with local schools, it can reach more teenagers and encourage them to take up the sport. The program has already attracted a diverse group of teenagers, and the gym's management hopes to see more success in the future.
